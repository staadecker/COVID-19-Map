About this map

This map was created to visualze residential areas in which there is a higher density of individuals who have symptoms of Coronavirus. The map has two layers: "confirmed" and "in self-isolation".

The "confirmed" layer shows flags where individuals have tested positive of COVID-19, while "in self-isolation" layer depicts regions with numbers of self-isolating individuals.
